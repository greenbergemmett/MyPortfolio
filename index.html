About Me

I am an Urban Informatics master's student at Northeastern University with a passion for using data-driven insights to shape more sustainable, efficient, and resilient cities. My interests lie at the intersection of transportation, sustainability, and climate resilience, where I explore how technology and analytics can drive smarter urban planning decisions. With experience in GIS, data visualization, and predictive modeling, I enjoy working with complex datasets to uncover trends and inform policy solutions. Whether analyzing traffic patterns, evaluating transit accessibility, or assessing climate impacts on urban infrastructure, I strive to leverage data to create meaningful change. I am always eager to collaborate on projects that promote equitable and sustainable urban development. Let’s connect!
